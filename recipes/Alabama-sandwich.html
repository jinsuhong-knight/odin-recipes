Alabama Style Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

For the sandwich

  • Shredded cooked chicken–use a rotisserie chicken or make your own
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Hamburger buns-or Brioche buns
  • Butter (optional)

For the sauce

  • Mayonnaise-use real mayonnaise
  • Cream style horseradish-different than prepared horseradish
  • Yellow mustard–for extra heat, try spicy brown mustard
  • Apple cider vinegar-white vinegar will also work
  • Sugar
  • Cayenne powder
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

Cooking chicken breasts for shredded chicken

You can find steps online for doing this in a slow cooker or Instant Pot, but here’s a simple method you can use:
  1. Season– Place two chicken breasts in a large pot and add salt and pepper
  2. Cover with water– Fill the pot about an inch above the meat.
  3. Poach-Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 8-10 minutes or until cooked through. Add 4 minutes to the cooking time for each additional breast, if needed.
  4. Shred– Remove the chicken from the pot and shred.
  5. In a bowl add the shredded chicken and Alabama White Sauce. Toss to coat and set aside.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns until golden brown. Spread butter on (optional).
  7. Place 1 cup of the chicken mixture on the bottom of a bun, top with 4 dill pickle slices and place top bun half on top.
